Instructions

General:

  • Don't touch any asteroids and make sure to stay inside the screen

Key Mode:

  • Hold the up arrow to increase power to the main thruster
  • Hold the right arrow to increase power to the right thruster
  • Hold the left arrow to increase power to the left thruster
  • Release keys to decrease thrust
  • Press P to pause/unpause the game

Touch Mode:

  • Swipe your finger continuously in the direction of the desired thruster
    • To power the main thruster swipe in the direction that the ship is pointing
    • To power the right thruster swipe to the right of where the ship is pointing
    • To power the left thruster swipe to the left of where the ship is pointing
